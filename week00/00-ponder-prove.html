TODO DOK

Pick a number (a positive integer). Form the product of that number, one more than that number, and one more than twice that number. Repeat for several different numbers. There is a pattern to these products. What is it?

Rephrase

Take a number(3), one higher than it(4), and double it plus one(7) Multiply them together(84). Repeat with different numbers and find the pattern.

Approach

1 * 2 * 3 = 6 /6 1 2 * 3 * 5 = 30 /6 5 3 * 4 * 7 = 84 /6 14 4 * 5 * 9 = 180 /6 30 5 * 6 * 11 = 330 /6 55 6 * 7 * 13 = 546 /6 91 7 * 8 * 15 = 840 /6 140

Solution

These numbers are all divisable by 6

Proof

n * (n + 1) * ((2 * n) + 1) (n2 + n) * (2n + 1) or (n + 1) * (2n2 + n)

(2n3 + 2n2 + n2 + n) (2n3 + 3n2 + n) n * (2n2 + 3n + 1)
